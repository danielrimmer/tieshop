A Heritage of Precision

Our story begins not in a boardroom, but at a cutting table in the heart of Como, where the world's finest silk is woven into existence. For generations, this region has been the global epicenter of silk craftsmanship. It is this legacy we honor with every fold, stitch, and press.

We believe in patience, precision, and the profound beauty of an object made by hand. Our ties are not mass-produced; they are brought to life by artisans who have dedicated their lives to this singular craft.

The Seven-Fold

The pinnacle of neckwear, our seven-fold ties are constructed from a single, generous piece of silk, folded seven times to create a tie of unmatched body and drape, requiring no liner.
